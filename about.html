// OUR STORY

SUKISH ENTERTAINMENT was founded in 2001 by visionary producer Kim Suk-Jin with a singular mission: to revolutionize the K-Pop industry through innovation, artistry, and cutting-edge technology.

What began as a small studio in the heart of Seoul has grown into one of the most influential entertainment companies in Asia, home to some of the most iconic artists of the past two decades.

// OUR MISSION

To push the boundaries of music and performance, nurturing talent that transcends cultural barriers and creates lasting connections with fans worldwide. We believe in the power of sound to shape the future.

// OUR VISION

By 2030, we aim to be the leading force in global entertainment, pioneering new technologies in music production, virtual performances, and artist development. The future is not something we wait for — it's something we create.
